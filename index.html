Who Am I?


Change is everything, and how I pivot comprises the core of my character. Working in a multitude of industries across a relatively short period of time has given me a unique perspective on solving a variety of problems. From an Amazon warehouse to boarding horses to running the operations of two hotels to freelance photography; my experiences have curated my skill sets to accomplish creating, operating and growing a variety of businesses. Professional goals include disrupting traditional organizations to form better employees and teams, serve customers more efficiently and work with communities as a whole — wherever they are located. Long-term, my passion is to help individuals pivot trauma-based life experiences into life-changing, impactful and meaningful purpose. Proudly in a Web Development cohort at Eleven Fifty Academy -based out of Indianapolis, IN.
